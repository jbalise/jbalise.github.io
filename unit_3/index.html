Julie Balise

Digital journalist specializing in business and tech.

Julie is a Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist whose work has been featured on SFGATE, MSN Money, Boston.com and beyond. Her skills include:

  • Reporting, writing and editing
  • Online content management
  • SEO
  • Social media management
  • Photo and video editing
  • Basic HTML and CSS

Journalism

Julie is known for launching the Office Space series at the San Francisco Chronicle. The column gave online and print readers a look inside Bay Area businesses, from startups to tech giants, like Google and Facebook. It ran for about a year. Read her column here.

Julie started her journalism career as a reporter for the Gatehouse Media New England, where she spent a year covering crime, town politics and feature stories. Read work from that time.

Julie helped launch BostonGlobe.com in 2011. She was an overnight producer responsible for updating the homepage, news and sports sections. She later joined Boston.com, where she contributed to the Pulitzer Prize-winning coverage of the Boston Marathon bombing. Read some of Julie's work from her time at the Globe.

Running

Julie became a runner in spring 2005 because all her friends were doing it. Ten years later, she's still out there. Julie most enjoyes 10Ks, but she's happy to run a 5K or longer distance. Read more about Julie's running here.

About Julie

Julie is a Massachusetts native who attended university at Northeastern University in Boston. She majored in journalism, with minors in international affairs and history. Julie graduated summa cum laude. She was deeply involve din the student newspaper and also contributed to the concert and marching bands' trumpet section. She studied abroad for one semester in Paris.
